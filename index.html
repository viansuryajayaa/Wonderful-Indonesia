Republic of Indonesia

Indonesia, country located off the coast of mainland Southeast Asia in the Indian and Pacific oceans. It is an archipelago that lies across the Equator and spans a distance equivalent to one-eighth of Earth’s circumference. The capital is Jakarta, located near the northwestern coast of Java.

Bhineka Tunggal ika

The word bhinnêka means "variety" and consists of the words bhinna and ika , which are combined.
The word tunggal means "one." The word ika means "it."
Literally, Bhinneka Tunggal Ika is translated as "Unity in Diversity"
